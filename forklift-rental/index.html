Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with NISHIO.

What types of equipment can I rent from NISHIO? +
We offer a comprehensive range including diesel forklifts, electric forklifts (lead acid & lithium ion), LPG forklifts, reach trucks, pallet trucks, sweepers, and scrubbers. Capacities range from 1.5 ton to 25 ton, suitable for warehouses, factories, ports, and construction sites.
What is the minimum rental period? +
Our standard minimum rental period is 3 months. For longer commitments (6 months, 1 year, or more), we offer more competitive monthly rates. The longer you rent, the more cost-effective it becomes.
Is maintenance really included in the rental price? +
Yes — all scheduled servicing, repairs, and replacement parts are fully covered in your rental fee. Our team of 42 skilled technicians handles everything so you can focus on running your business. No hidden costs or surprise invoices.
What happens if my rented forklift breaks down? +
We provide a standby replacement unit to minimise downtime. Our service team responds quickly, and if a repair takes time, a backup forklift is deployed to keep your operations running smoothly.
How quickly can I get a forklift delivered? +
Once your rental agreement is confirmed, delivery is typically within 3–5 working days depending on your location and equipment availability. For urgent requirements, please contact us directly via WhatsApp for the fastest response.
Can I adjust my fleet size during the rental period? +
Absolutely. We offer flexible rental arrangements that allow you to scale up or down based on your changing business needs. Whether you need additional units for a busy season or want to reduce your fleet, we'll work with you.
Which areas in Malaysia do you serve? +
We serve Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, Negeri Sembilan, Malacca, Johor, Penang, and Perak (Ipoh area). Our head office is in Shah Alam with support teams across Peninsular Malaysia.
